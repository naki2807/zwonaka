Vision Statement

Reclaiming lives, restoring hope and guiding homeless people from streets to stability through rehabilitation.


Mission Statement

To rescue, rehabilitate, and reintegrate homeless people struggling with addiction, providing a comprehensive support system for sustainable recovery and reintegration into society.


Aim

The aim is to get homeless people back on their feet and more likely back to their normal lives. It ensures to get them clean by taking them to rehabilitation centers to help them with their addiction.
